Our Methodology

Dual-Engine Growth

We bridge the gap between passive indexing and active hedge fund management by utilizing a rules-based approach to leverage and volatility.

Leveraged ETFs

We utilize Leveraged ETFs (LETFs) to gain enhanced exposure to broad market indices. Rather than using margin debt which incurs call risk, we use the structural leverage inherent in these instruments to target 2x-3x daily equity exposure during favorable trend regimes.

Index-Based Options

We deploy a systematic options-based strategy win a high win rate and tight risk controls to maximize returns.
